= Pillar 6 - Sustainability

== What is it about?

Sustainability means designing IT architectures and processes to be resource-efficient and environmentally friendly. In WAF++, sustainability is a strategic factor.

== What is being done?

* *Energy efficiency:* Using energy-optimized hardware and cloud regions.
* *Resource utilization optimization:* Avoiding over-provisioning.
* *Green IT:* Using sustainable technologies and supply chains.

== What needs to be considered?

* *Transparency:* Measuring CO₂ footprint and energy consumption.
* *Regulatory requirements:* Compliance with ESG mandates.
* *Awareness:* Sustainability must be part of the organizational culture.

== Where is this headed?

* *Carbon-neutral IT:* Reducing and offsetting emissions.
* *Automated optimization:* Dynamic adjustment for minimal environmental impact.
* *Sustainability as a KPI:* Sustainability becomes a strategic success factor.
