      <p class="waf-muted">Each pillar is divided into 5 maturity levels – from initial baseline measures to fully automated, measurable excellence.</p>
      <ul class="waf-bullets" style="margin:12px 0 0;">
        <li><strong>Level 1 – Initial:</strong> Minimal measures, manual, reactive</li>

        <li><strong>Level 3 – Defined:</strong> Standardized processes, clear responsibilities</li>
        <li><strong>Level 4 – Managed:</strong> Measurability, KPIs, continuous improvement</li>
        <li><strong>Level 5 – Optimizing:</strong> Automated, predictive, fully auditable</li>
