= Pillar 5 - Operational Excellence

== What is it about?

Operational Excellence in WAF++ means designing processes to be efficient, transparent, and automated. The goal is to keep operations stable and scalable while fostering innovation.

== What is being done?

* *Automation:* CI/CD pipelines, infrastructure as code.
* *Monitoring & observability:* Full transparency over systems and processes.
* *Incident management:* Clear processes for incidents and escalations.
* *Documentation:* Consistent and up-to-date documentation for all teams.

== What needs to be considered?

* *Standardization:* Consistent tools and processes.
* *Continuous improvement:* Regular reviews and retrospectives.
* *Training:* Teams must be empowered to understand and apply processes.

== Where is this headed?

* *Hyper-automation:* Maximum automation of operational processes.
* *Data-driven operations:* Decisions based on real-time data.
* *DevOps culture:* Seamless collaboration between development and operations.
