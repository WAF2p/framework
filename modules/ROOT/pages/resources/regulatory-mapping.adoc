= Regulatory Mapping
:description: Which WAF++ controls cover which compliance requirements – GDPR, BSI C5, ISO 27001, EUCS, GAIA-X, FinOps
:toc: left
:toclevels: 3

This page gives compliance officers and auditors a direct overview:
which WAF++ controls cover which regulatory requirements – cross-pillar, in a single view.

[TIP]
====
The mappings reflect the normative intent of the controls, but do not replace legal advice.
A complete compliance statement always requires an organization-specific gap analysis.
====
