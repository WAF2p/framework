== Recommended Order

For organizations just getting started with Performance Efficiency:

. *xref:best-practices/bp-observability.adoc[Performance Observability & SLOs]* –
  Measure first. Without SLOs and metrics, all other best practices are blind.

. *xref:best-practices/bp-database-performance.adoc[Optimize Database Performance]* –
  Database optimization typically has the highest impact per unit of time.

. *xref:best-practices/bp-autoscaling.adoc[Configure Auto-Scaling]* –
  Set up and validate elasticity for variable loads.

. *xref:best-practices/bp-caching.adoc[Implement Caching Strategy]* –
  Eliminate redundant database accesses through caching.

. *xref:best-practices/bp-load-testing.adoc[Load Tests & Stress Tests]* –
  Integrate performance validation into the deployment process.

. *xref:best-practices/bp-compute-sizing.adoc[Compute Sizing & Instance Selection]* –
  Optimize instance types based on measured data.

. *xref:best-practices/bp-network-performance.adoc[Optimize Network Performance]* –
  Reduce network latency and optimize routing.
